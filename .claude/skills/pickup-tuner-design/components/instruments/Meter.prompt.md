The level meter — a vintage dual-needle VU on an ivory dial face. The dark RMS needle rests on the level; a thinner brass peak-hold needle holds the highest peak; the arc's top end is an oxblood hot zone.

```jsx
<Meter rms={-14.2} hold={-3.1} />
```

- All inputs are dBFS (floor −60). Redlining here is meaningful — a hot signal pushes the needle into the oxblood zone near 0 dBFS.
- Pair with a "Level" `Panel` and the mono peak/hold/rms readout above it.
